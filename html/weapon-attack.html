Weapon Attack Instructions

Rapier (Finesse Weapon)

Attack Roll: Roll a d20 and add your Dexterity modifier (+5). Add your proficiency bonus (+3) if proficient. Compare the result to the target's AC. If it equals or exceeds the target’s AC, the attack hits.
Damage Roll: On a hit, roll 1d8 for damage and add your Dexterity modifier (+5).

Shortbow (Ranged Weapon)

Attack Roll: Roll a d20 and add your Dexterity modifier (+5). Add your proficiency bonus (+3) if proficient. Compare the result to the target's AC. If it equals or exceeds the target’s AC, the attack hits.
Damage Roll: On a hit, roll 1d6 for damage and add your Dexterity modifier (+5).

Daggers (Thrown Weapon)

Attack Roll: Roll a d20 and add your Dexterity modifier (+5). Add your proficiency bonus (+3) if proficient. Compare the result to the target's AC. If it equals or exceeds the target’s AC, the attack hits.
Damage Roll: On a hit, roll 1d4 for damage and add your Dexterity modifier (+5). You can throw a dagger within range (20 ft. normal, 60 ft. long range).
