Class Features (Rogue)

Sneak Attack: 2d6
Cunning Action: Dash, Disengage, or Hide as a bonus action
Uncanny Dodge: Halve damage from attacks you can see
Roguish Archetype: Thief:
  • Fast Hands: Use your bonus action to make a Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) check, use thieves' tools, or interact with an object
  • Second-Story Work: Climb at your walking speed, and your jumping distance increases
